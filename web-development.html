CanWe


According to the Global Hunger Index (GHI), Indonesia ranks third highest in hunger levels in Southeast Asia in 2021. Indonesia received a GHI score of 18 points, categorizing it as a moderate level. The GHI describes a country's hunger situation in relation to the basic physiological needs of humans, including food and nutrition. The GHI index score is based on four components: undernourishment, child wasting, child stunting, and child mortality.


CanWe is here to help address the challenges faced by the Indonesian community in meeting their food and nutrition needs. CanWe is a platform aimed at collecting donations and fundraising to improve the quality of life for children and families in need by providing healthy food and nutritional education. Through CanWe, we hope to ensure that more children not only survive but thrive today and in the future. Can We? Yes, We Can!


Features

  • Donate
  • Fundraising
  • Notifications
  • Profile
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Roles

  • Users have the authority to access the landing page, visit the profile page, make donations or fundraising, receive notifications, and send questions to the website administrators.
  • Moderators act as the controllers of the website and have full access to moderate the content present on CanWe.

APAPEDIA


PT XYZ, the company behind the bacabaca application, wants to expand its business. Executives at PT XYZ see a promising business opportunity in the online transaction or e-commerce sector, which they consider to be the most strategic and rapidly growing economic sector at present. E-commerce can be a significant opportunity for SMEs to expand their businesses. In response to this demand, PT XYZ has instructed your team to develop the APAPEDIA application.


In this task, you are required to implement APAPEDIA using your knowledge both from classes and outside of classes, utilizing the Spring Boot framework as the back-end and another framework as the mobile front-end. For this occasion, you will develop the mobile UI using Flutter. Flutter is an SDK tool used for developing Android, iOS, web, and even desktop applications. In processing and displaying data in Flutter, you will need assistance from app state management. For this task, you are free to choose app state management such as Flutter Bloc, GetX, Provider, etc.


APAPEDIA is a mini-commerce application that implements microservices. There are three services in this application:

  1. User Service: Manages user-related functionalities.
  2. Catalog Service: Manages products in the mini-commerce.
  3. Order Service: Manages orders in the mini-commerce, including the implementation of a shopping cart.
